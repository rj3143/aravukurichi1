About MLA

Thiru. Elangovan is the elected Member of Legislative Assembly (MLA) from Aravakurichi Constituency and a dedicated leader of Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK). With a strong commitment to public welfare, social justice, and inclusive development, he actively works for the progress of the people of Aravakurichi.

His mission is to ensure that every citizen benefits from government welfare schemes, infrastructure growth, employment opportunities, and quality education. He stands as a true representative of the people, always approachable and service-minded.

Vision & Mission

People First Governance

To ensure every citizen receives equal respect, equal opportunity and access to welfare schemes.

Infrastructure Development

Strengthening roads, drinking water supply, drainage systems, and modern facilities in villages and towns.

Youth Empowerment

Supporting students and youth through education guidance, employment awareness, and skill development programs.

Social Justice

Protecting equality and dignity for all communities by following DMK principles and Dravidian ideology.
