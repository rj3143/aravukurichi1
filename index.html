About MLA Elangovan

Thiru. Elangovan is a committed and service-minded political leader of the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) and the elected Member of Legislative Assembly (MLA) representing the people of Aravakurichi Constituency in Karur District, Tamil Nadu.

With a strong belief in social justice, equality, and inclusive growth, he continuously works to improve infrastructure, education, healthcare access, rural development, and employment opportunities. He actively engages with the public, listens to their grievances, and ensures that government welfare schemes reach the deserving citizens.

His leadership reflects the values of the Dravidian movement, focusing on dignity, development, and democratic governance. As a public representative, his primary objective is to serve the people with transparency, responsibility, and dedication.

Constituency Development Activities

Aravakurichi constituency development is based on long-term planning and public needs assessment. Thiru. Elangovan ensures that every village and town receives equal priority in development works. Key focus areas include road improvements, water supply, sanitation, public buildings, educational institutions, and rural employment growth.

Through constant monitoring and coordination with government departments, the MLA office works to accelerate approvals and ensure timely completion of development projects. Special importance is given to public safety, rural infrastructure, and basic facilities for the common people.

Road & Transport Development

  • Improvement of rural roads and village connectivity
  • Maintenance and repair of damaged road sections
  • Better access roads to schools, hospitals, and markets

Water & Sanitation Projects

  • Drinking water pipeline expansion
  • Borewell support and water tank renovation
  • Drainage cleaning and sanitation awareness

Public Infrastructure

  • Street light installation and maintenance
  • Community hall improvement projects
  • Government building renovation support

Government Welfare & Public Service

The Government of Tamil Nadu under the leadership of DMK is implementing multiple welfare schemes for women, farmers, students, workers, and senior citizens. MLA Thiru. Elangovan actively ensures that these schemes reach every deserving beneficiary in Aravakurichi constituency.

The MLA office supports the public by providing guidance for scheme applications, document preparation, grievance submission, and follow-up with government departments.

Women Welfare & Empowerment

Support for women self-help groups, welfare assistance, entrepreneurship development, and guidance for government schemes designed to empower women economically and socially.

Farmer Support & Agriculture Growth

Awareness programs for farmers, subsidy guidance, irrigation support, agricultural loan facilitation, and promotion of modern farming techniques for rural prosperity.

Education & Student Welfare

Scholarship awareness, school development initiatives, student counseling programs, and support for government education schemes ensuring equal opportunities.

Healthcare & Medical Camps

Organizing medical camps, helping citizens access health insurance schemes, and supporting hospital facility improvement in the constituency.
